Who We Are

The Bridge Between
Technology & Territory

Emirates Projects & Development (EPD) is a Morocco-based development and project coordination company, founded by UAE and Moroccan principals with deep roots in both markets.

We act as the local contracting and integration platform for innovative housing technologies in Morocco — managing government relations, regulatory processes, project structuring, and partner coordination so that international technology companies can enter the market with confidence and clarity.

Our authorized representatives bring over 20 years of EPC and international infrastructure project experience, including major programmes across the Middle East, North Africa, and beyond.

"Our platform combines international engineering experience with local project structuring capability — enabling efficient execution within complex regulatory environments."

The Market

Why Morocco — Why Now

01
National Housing Deficit
Morocco faces a significant unmet demand for housing across all segments. The government has made accelerated, scalable construction a national priority, creating direct opportunities for proven technology partners.
02
2030 FIFA World Cup Mandate
Morocco is co-hosting the 2030 World Cup, driving unprecedented investment in infrastructure, hospitality, and residential development across major cities. Speed is essential.
03
Regulatory Receptiveness
The Moroccan regulatory environment is increasingly open to innovative construction technologies. Morocco was among the first African nations to pilot 3D-printed structures, signalling institutional readiness.
04
Gateway to Africa
A successful Morocco programme positions technology partners advantageously across West and North Africa — one of the world's fastest-growing construction markets.
⚽ FIFA World Cup 2030
Morocco at a Historic Inflection Point
The Kingdom of Morocco is entering one of the most significant infrastructure investment cycles in its history. Co-hosting the 2030 FIFA World Cup alongside Spain and Portugal demands rapid delivery of housing, hospitality, and civic infrastructure at unprecedented scale.
For 3D construction technology companies, this creates a rare window: the combination of urgency, government commitment, and market scale that turns pilot projects into full programmes — with a local partner already positioned at the interface of government and delivery.
Al Haouz Reconstruction

Morocco's 2023 earthquake created an additional humanitarian mandate for fast, resilient, scalable reconstruction in Al Haouz Province — a programme where 3D construction technology offers compelling advantages in both speed and structural integrity.
